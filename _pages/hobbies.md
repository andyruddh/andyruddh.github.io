### Music

I produce electronic music under the alias [Andy-Ruddh](https://soundcloud.com/andyruddh) and my sounds consist of raw and hypnotic techno. My music has been supported by pioneers of this genre such as **Richie Hawtin, P.E.A.R.L., Wata Igarashi, Oliver Rosemann, Shlomi Aber**, and many more. My music has placed within the Top 50 on the Beatport Global Techno charts in 2023 and 2024.

I also co-moderate a music-curation community of truly authentic Techno music at [r/ProperTechno](https://reddit.com/r/ProperTechno/).

### Tennis

I was previously a junior tennis player in the Indian and Asian circuits. I continue playing recreationally.
