hobbies

my music and tennis

I produce electronic music under the alias Andy-Ruddh and my sounds consist of raw and hypnotic techno. My music has been supported by Richie Hawtin, a pioneer of this genre. My label-debut EP reached its highest ranking of #31 on the Beatport Global Techno charts in February 2023.

I was previously a junior tennis player in the Indian and Asian circuits.
